# Online Demo

The SQLFlow widget is shipped together with [the SQLFlow On-Premise version](https://www.gudusoft.com/sqlflow-on-premise-version/).&#x20;

Once the SQLFlow widget is installed on your server, you can access the SQLFlow widget with the url such as: [https://127.0.0.1/widget](https://127.0.0.1/widget). Currently there is no available online demo.
